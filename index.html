Editor's Take

The masa program alone makes this place worth the trip. Upscale without feeling pretentious, and the bar team is doing some of the most interesting work in the city right now. Not cheap, but you'll leave feeling like you got your money's worth.

What to Order
  • Suerte Tortillas (always)
  • Wagyu Barbacoa
  • Any seasonal masa dish
What to Skip
  • The churros (just fine, not special)
